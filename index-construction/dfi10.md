# DFI10

The DeFi Index 10 (DFI10) index is an equal-weighted index with 10 constituents selected from the Decentralized Finance Industry Group in CICS.

## Ticker

CBIDFI10.

## Calculation Methodology

Equal Weighted.

## Base Date

2021-05-17.

## Base Level

100\.
